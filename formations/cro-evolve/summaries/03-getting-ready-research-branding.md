# 03. Getting Ready To Do Research + Branding

## Concept Central
**Infrastructure pré-research : docs de base + brand alignment** — Avant de commencer le research phase complète, préparer 3 documents fondamentaux : brand guidelines (vision/mission/values), onboarding self, research doc. Ces docs structurent le research et alignent la stratégie.

## Points Clés
- **3 Documents Requis** :
  1. **Brand Guidelines** (Canva template fourni) :
     - Vision, mission, brand values
     - Brand voice + personality
     - Customer personas
     - Visual identity (colors, typography, logo)
     - Brand history
     - Competitive positioning
  2. **Onboarding Self** (11 questions) :
     - What is your differentiator?
     - 2-3 things you wish everyone knew
     - Best-selling products
     - How do you want to be perceived?
  3. **Research Document** (brain dump + organized sections) :
     - Headline ideas, visual ideas, creative concepts
     - Company info (who, what, why)
     - Product functions vs benefits
     - Competitive advantages, use cases, reviews
     - Customer needs/wants/pain points
     - Competitor research section
     - Creative audit section

- **Critical mindset** :
  - "Why am I doing this in the first place?"
  - Brand without why = people see through it
  - Research without brand guidelines = scattered insights
  - $200K+/month brands CAN exist sans guidelines, mais → scaling friction

- **Not required** :
  - Don't spend 3 days perfecting brand guidelines
  - Start research even if guides incomplete
  - Build guidelines iteratively (weekend project, not blockers)

- **Links structure** (in research doc) :
  - Link to onboarding doc
  - Link to brand guidelines
  - Link to Foreplay vision board (competitor swipes)

- **Tools** :
  - Foreplay.co ($49/month, 7-day free trial) : ad swipe library
  - Google Sheets templates : research + competitor doc

## Insights
- Most founders skip brand questions and lose momentum → "I don't even have a reason why"
- Research without brand strategy = ideas without direction
- Brand guidelines = long-term asset (builds trust, aligns team, scales faster)
- "Why" is often the missing link in failed ads
- Research phase is 30% internal clarity + 70% external market listening

## Applications drinknellio.com
- **Brand Guidelines pour Nellio** (to create) :
  - **Vision** : "#1 natural anxiety solution for Germans seeking sleep without drugs"
  - **Mission** : "Help 1M Germans reclaim sleep + calmness via scientifically-backed poudre"
  - **Values** : Transparency (ingredient science), Results (45-day guarantee), Community (testimonials)
  - **Voice** : Calm authority (educational, not salesy), German vernacular (not formal business)
  - **Persona Core** : Stressed German professionals, 25-45, earning €50K+, skeptical of supplements
  
- **Onboarding Self (Chef perspective)** :
  - Differentiator : 300mg Ashwagandha + L-Theanin combo (vs generics 150mg)
  - Wish everyone knew : 45-day money-back = zero risk, 20K+ 4.8★ reviews = proven
  - Best-seller : Poudre Framboise-Citron (vs caps/tablets)
  - Perception desired : Premium natural solution, not commodity

- **Research Doc structure** :
  - Company = drinknellio.com (anxiety DTC)
  - Products = UltraCalm only (focus, not line extension)
  - Competitors direct = Ashwagandha brands (Amazon)
  - Competitors indirect = Melatonin + prescription sleep
  - Customer day-to-day struggles = insomnia + anxiety + focus

- **Foreplay setup** : 
  - Spy on Ashwagandha competitors' ads
  - Monitor health supplement campaigns
  - Collect 20-30 winning ad swipes

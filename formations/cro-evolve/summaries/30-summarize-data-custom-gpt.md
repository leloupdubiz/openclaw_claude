# How To Summarize All Data & Create a Custom GPT — Résumé CRO

## 🎯 Concept Central
Un **Custom GPT trained on customer data** = customer-obsessed AI qui connaît tous tes clients intimement. Upload tous les fichiers (feedback, reviews, website data), configure prompts conversationnels, et partage avec ton équipe + clients pour queries instantanées.

## 📌 Points Clés
- **Avantages du multi-file vs. single-file approach**
  - Single file : summarize 1 question à la fois (plus long)
  - Multi-file : upload tout, ask globalement (plus efficace)
  - Recommendation : upload tout dans 1 GPT instance

- **File preparation**
  - Export written answers seulement (pas multiple choice)
  - Créer new sheet, paste answers
  - Download as PDF
  - Rename par question (ex: "improved_store_warroad.pdf")

- **GPT Configuration**
  - Upload tous les PDFs dans 1 GPT
  - Logo du client (nice touch)
  - **Conversation starters** : pre-populate des questions clés
    - "What do customers want to see next?"
    - "What can be improved?"
    - "What products should we make?"
    - "What do customers tell friends about us?" (si "tell a friend" Q exists)

- **Sharing + Usage**
  - Copy GPT link → envoie à team + clients
  - Clients peuvent poser questions directement (super value add)
  - Team can ask strategic questions anytime
  - Regenerate answers = automatique (GPT gère)

- **Visual enhancements**
  - Client logo upload
  - Bold titles dans summaries
  - Color coding pas nécessaire mais optionnel

## 💡 Insights Appliqués

| Scenario | Implementation |
|----------|----------------|
| 4 written questions | 4 PDFs upload, 1 GPT instance |
| Feedback only | Upload feedback PDFs |
| Feedback + reviews | Upload 2 file types, single GPT |
| Team collaboration | Share link à team; they can query |

## 🍹 Applications drinknellio.com

1. **Nellio Customer GPT Setup**
   - **Name** : "Nellio Customer Knowledge Hub"
   - **Description** : "This GPT knows everything about Nellio UltraCalm customers, their needs, pain points, and what they love."
   - **Files to upload**
     - Giveaway feedback PDF (written answers only)
     - Amazon reviews PDF (if available)
     - Reddit mentions (if any)
   - **Instructions** : "You are an expert in Nellio customer psychology. Answer based on uploaded documents about their calm-seeking journey, stress relief needs, and product experience."

2. **Conversation starters custom pour Nellio**
   - "What are customers' top pain points before discovering Nellio?"
   - "What results are customers reporting after 1 week of use?"
   - "Why do customers recommend Nellio to friends?"
   - "What competitors do customers mention, and why do they prefer Nellio?"
   - "What flavors or formats would customers want to see?"
   - "How quickly do customers feel the calm effect?"

3. **Workflow post-feedback collection**
   - Week 1 : Collect giveaway entrants (1300+)
   - Week 2 : Export, organize, upload to GPT
   - Week 3+ : Team + client ask questions, iterate messaging
   - Monthly : Re-upload new feedback (keep GPT fresh)

4. **Output example**
   - Query : "Based on customer feedback, what are the top 3 reasons they switch from coffee to Nellio?"
   - GPT generates : "1. No jitters (20 mentions) 2. Better focus (15 mentions) 3. Longer-lasting (12 mentions)"
   - → Input direct pour Hook Writer (Agent 3)

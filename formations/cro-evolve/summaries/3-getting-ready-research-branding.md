# Getting Ready To Do Research + Branding — Résumé CRO

## 🎯 Concept Central
Avant research marché, préparer ton **research repository** : document partagé qui consolide tout (brand guidelines, onboarding, research findings, competitor audit, creative ideas). C'est ton **brain dump organísé** pour la stratégie.

## 📌 Points Clés
- **Setup du Research Doc (document partagé)**
  - Copy template fourni
  - Update company name
  - Quick links vers brand guidelines + onboarding (voir ci-dessous)
  
- **Brand Guidelines** (optionnel mais important)
  - Template Canva fourni (gratuit)
  - 11 sections : mission, vision, values, brand voice, persona, colors, typography, logo, history
  - Pas obligatoire d'en avoir pour démarrer (clients doing $200K+/month sans)
  - Projet weekend = fais-le, mais pas blocker pour research
  
- **Onboarding document (obligatoire)**
  - 11 questions d'auto-assessment
  - Product differentiators
  - Brand perception souhaitée
  - Best-sellers identifiés
  - Pain points clients
  - Day-to-day struggles

- **Research Document sections**
  - Company info (who, products, functions, features, benefits, competitive advantages, use cases, reviews)
  - Competitor research (direct + indirect)
  - Creative audit (ton ad account actuel)
  - Brain dump (headlines, visual ideas, creative concepts as you discover)

## 💡 Insights Appliqués

| Section | Purpose |

## 🍹 Applications drinknellio.com

1. **Créer Nellio Research Doc**
   - Company: Nellio UltraCalm, Ashwagandha/L-Theanin focus, Marché DE
   - Products: Poudre framboise-citron primaire; autres flaveurs
   - Differentiators: 300mg ashwagandha (premium), 45j money-back, 4.8★
   - Competitors: Direct (Magnesium generics), Indirect (Melatonin, Kava)

2. **Onboarding 11 questions**
   - Q1: Differentiators → Premium ingredients + fast-acting
   - Q2: What should market know? → Clinically proven calm, no grogginess
   - Q3: Best sellers → Poudre original (volume), possible flaveur #2
   - Q4: Brand perception → Trust + efficacy + Allemand market

3. **Start brain dump**
   - As you research, note :
     - Competitor hooks : "Stress melts in 15 min", "Non-habit forming"
     - Visual ideas : Meditation vibes, sleep, focus sequences
     - Avatar insights : Professionals, moms, students from Twitter

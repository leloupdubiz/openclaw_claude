# Understanding Your Customer

## Core Concept
Understanding your customer is the foundation of creating effective ads, as it allows you to tailor your message and approach to their specific desires, fears, and problems. By knowing your customer inside and out, you can create ads that feel like they were made specifically for them, increasing the chances of conversion. This involves going beyond broad demographics and instead creating a detailed picture of your ideal customer.

## Key Points
* 99% of marketers focus on the wrong aspects of ad creation, such as hooks, editing styles, and music, rather than understanding their customer.
* Creating high-converting ads starts with building a detailed picture of your customer, including their desires, fears, and problems.
* Understanding your customer's language, values, and behavior is crucial in creating effective ads.
* Research techniques, such as analyzing social media comments and Amazon reviews, can help you gather information about your customer.
* Creating a customer avatar can help you visualize and understand your ideal customer.
* Understanding your customer changes everything about how you create ads, from the format to the tone and language used.
* Most editors spend too much time on editing and not enough time understanding their customer, resulting in ineffective ads.

## Actionable Steps
1. **Identify a specific customer avatar**: Instead of targeting a broad demographic, choose a specific type of person you're selling to, such as successful moms who feel guilty about not having enough time for their kids.
2. **Conduct research**: Gather information about your customer by observing their behavior online, analyzing social media comments, and reading reviews on platforms like Amazon.
3. **Create a detailed customer avatar**: Based on your research, create a detailed picture of your ideal customer, including their desires, fears, and values, and give them a name, face, and story to make them feel real.

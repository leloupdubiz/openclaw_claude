# My Goal Is To Change Your Life

## Core Concept
The core concept of this lesson is that the instructor, Carlo, aims to change the lives of his students by teaching them valuable skills, particularly in e-commerce and paid advertising. He emphasizes that making money should not be the primary goal, but rather acquiring skills that can lead to financial freedom and a successful career. By stacking multiple skills, individuals can increase their income potential and become more valuable to businesses.

## Key Points
* Carlo's primary goal is to help students make back their investment in the program as soon as possible.
* His secondary goal is to change their lives by teaching them a mindset and skills that can lead to financial freedom.
* Chasing money is the wrong approach; instead, individuals should focus on acquiring skills.
* Stacking multiple skills can lead to exponential growth in income potential.
* The skills learned in the program, such as video editing, design, and psychology, can be combined to create a powerful skill set.
* With this skill set, individuals can make winning ads for e-commerce brands and increase their value to businesses.
* Having a skill set provides options and freedom, allowing individuals to choose their career path and work with various brands or start their own business.
* No one can take away a skill set, and it can be used to replicate success over and over again.

## Actionable Steps
1. Focus on acquiring skills rather than chasing money.
2. Identify the skills you need to learn to achieve your goals and start stacking them.
3. Practice and master each skill before moving on to the next one.
4. Combine your skills to create a powerful skill set that can be applied to various areas of e-commerce and paid advertising.
5. Use your skills to create winning ads for e-commerce brands and increase your value to businesses.

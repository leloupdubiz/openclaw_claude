# How You Will Turn Into a Top 5% Marketer

## Core Concept
To become a top 5% marketer, one must first learn how to learn, which involves adopting a different approach than traditional education. The program focuses on learning through action, feedback, and repetition, rather than mere consumption of information. This approach enables individuals to develop real skills that can be applied in the field of marketing and e-commerce.

## Key Points
* Traditional education often fails to provide effective learning, as it relies on memorization rather than practical application.
* Learning is defined as "same condition, different behavior," which means that individuals must apply new skills and knowledge to real-world situations in order to truly learn.
* The program provides a structured approach to learning, including learning, practicing, testing skills, and connecting with brands.
* Ad bounties, a unique feature of the program, allow individuals to test their skills by creating ads for real brands and earning passive income.
* Consistency and persistence are key to learning and improving marketing skills.
* The program provides a private job board with opportunities for individuals to connect with brands and apply their skills.

## Actionable Steps
1. **Learn**: Consume content and take notes, but recognize that this is only the first step in the learning process.
2. **Practice**: Apply new skills and knowledge to real-world situations, such as creating ads for brands.
3. **Test and Refine**: Continuously test and refine skills through ad bounties and other practical applications.

## Application for Nellio UltraCalm (German market)
To apply the principles of the program to a Meta Ads campaign for Nellio UltraCalm in the German market, one could:
* Create ads that target specific demographics and interests, and test their effectiveness through ad bounties.
* Use the program's job board to connect with brands and gain experience in creating ads for similar products.
* Focus on developing a deep understanding of the German market and the target audience for Nellio UltraCalm, and apply this knowledge to create effective ads.

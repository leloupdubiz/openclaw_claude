# Buying Psychology

## Core Concept
The core concept of buying psychology is that people don't buy products, they buy the fulfillment of their desires. As a marketer, your job is to understand these desires and position your product as the bridge to fulfilling them. This requires a deep understanding of human desire and how to channel it towards your product.

## Key Points
* People buy products to satisfy their desires, not just to purchase a product
* Desires are already present in the market, and marketers can't create new ones, only channel them towards their product
* The strength of desires varies, and understanding the dominant desire behind a product is key to effective marketing
* Every product has two aspects: the physical product and the transformational product (the change it brings to the customer's life)
* Features and benefits alone don't sell; it's the transformational product that resonates with customers
* Visual storytelling is more effective than text-based marketing, as the brain processes visual information 60,000 times faster
* To create effective ads, marketers should focus on showing the transformation that fulfills the customer's desire, rather than just listing product benefits

## Actionable Steps
1. Identify the dominant desire behind your product and understand how it fulfills that desire
2. Create a list of product benefits and connect each one to the desire it satisfies
3. Develop a plan to show the desire being fulfilled in your ads, using visual storytelling and real-life examples
4. Use exercises like the one demonstrated in the lesson to practice connecting product benefits to desires and developing ad concepts
5. Focus on the transformational product and the change it brings to the customer's life, rather than just the physical product

## Application for Nellio UltraCalm (German market)
For Nellio UltraCalm, an anti-stress drink brand in the German market, the dominant desire might be to feel calm and relaxed in a busy, stressful world. To fulfill this desire, the brand could create ads showing people enjoying a peaceful moment with Nellio UltraCalm, such as a busy professional unwinding after work or a student relaxing during exam season. The ads could focus on the transformational product, showcasing how Nellio UltraCalm helps people achieve a sense of calm and well-being, rather than just listing the product's ingredients or features.

## Key Quote
> "People don't buy products, they buy better versions of themselves."

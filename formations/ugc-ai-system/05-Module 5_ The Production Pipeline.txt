From Prompt to Video That Prints

We will use two primary AI-powered tools to generate high-quality videos for our products. 

The only current limitation is that we're restricted to a maximum video length of 15 seconds. 

I'm actively researching methods to extend Sora-generated videos beyond this timeframe, but so far I haven't been successful when working with real human faces in the footage…

If I discover a breakthrough or workaround, this guide will be immediately updated with the new methodology.

Our two main video generation tools:
	•	Higgsfield
	•	Kie.ai (60% cheaper than openai)

Important Warning: Don't use FREEPIK as your video generation tool because it imposes strict limits on the number of characters you can include in your prompts, which severely restricts the level of detail and specificity we need for professional results.

I believe the process will now become straightforward and easy to follow...
The Higgsfield WAY

For this demonstration example, I've used the Gruns product along with a reference video that I successfully cloned and adapted.

Here is the workflow i used: 1. Upload video to our custom gemini gem (project)
2. Tell Gemini to analyze the video and give me every detail etc… like how we did it before
3. Now we get the gemini prompt and send it to claude for final touch
4. Upload the product image, and give it the claude prompt
5. FINAL RESULTS

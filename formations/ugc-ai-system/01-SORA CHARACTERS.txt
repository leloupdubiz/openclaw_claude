HOW TO GENERATE CONSISTENT SORA 2 CHARACTERS
Step-by-step guide

Generating The Character

The way we will generate the consistent character in sora 2 is really simple.

First, pick one of the prompts you created earlier in the course. Any of the example prompts will work perfectly.

Here's the step-by-step workflow:

	•	1. Open the official Sora 2 app
	•	2. Create a video using your chosen prompt
	•	3. Once the video is ready, click the three dots menu
	•	4. Select "Generate Character"
	•	5. Publish the character and make it public
	•	6. Now you can reuse this character by tagging it in future prompts

In the sections below, I'll walk you through the complete workflow for using this character across multiple videos, plus how to keep your product placements consistent.

Consistent Character Across Multiple Videos

VERY IMPORTANT - PRODUCT PLACEMENT
To generate consistent product placements in your videos, you need to create the character holding the product directly in the Sora app. This approach guarantees the character will hold the product in every scene.

Why this matters: Sora 2's character generation doesn't follow uploaded images when it comes to product placement.

How to do it:
	•	Use the prepared prompt below to generate detailed product information
	•	Add this product description to your existing prompt

If you want to generate a scene without the character holding the product, just write on the prompt “she holds nothing on her hand”, or say to gemini/claude to remove the product.

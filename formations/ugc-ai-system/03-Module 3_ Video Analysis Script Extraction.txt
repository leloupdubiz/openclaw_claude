What Consistency Actually Means:
Not just "woman in her 20s with brown hair."

True consistency:
	•	Same eye shape, color, spacing
	•	Same nose structure, proportions
	•	Same jawline, face shape
	•	Same lip fullness, mouth width
	•	Same skin tone, texture
	•	Same subtle features (freckles, eye shape, smile lines)
When you nail this: You can generate 50 videos with the same "creator" and less than %1 knows they're not real.
The ROI
Inconsistent character approach:
	•	Every video = new face
	•	Can't build "creator brand"
	•	Each video stands alone
	•	Audience doesn't recognize continuity
Consistent character approach:
	•	Same "creator" across campaigns
	•	Builds familiarity and trust
	•	Creates the illusion of an actual person reviewing your product over time
Consistency = you're not just making videos, you're creating a virtual brand ambassador.

Hook Extraction & Iteration

The Strategy:
The hook is everything. 
Your Gem gives you the winning hook blueprint. 

Your job: iterate on the formula, not copy it.

Example: Problem-Solution Lead
Original Hook:

"You know how you can never fall asleep at night?"

Create 10 Variations:
	•	"Still staring at your ceiling at 2 AM? Here's the problem."
	•	"If you want to be asleep in the next 10 minutes, don't skip this."
	•	"There's a reason you can't sleep, and it has nothing to do with your mattress."
	•	"38,000 people fixed their sleep with this… here's how."
	•	"I changed one thing before bed. Now I'm asleep in 5 minutes."
	•	"Tired of losing hours staring at the dark? This ends tonight."
	•	"Your sleep problems get worse the longer you wait. Watch this."
	•	"Sleep doctors don't want you to know this simple trick."
	•	"If you're reading this at 1 AM, you need to see this."
	•	"Fall asleep faster tonight. No pills. No tricks. Just this."
The Competitive Advantage is that you’re testing ten variations of a proven winner.

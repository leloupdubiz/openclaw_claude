### 📚 CONTENU DÉTAILLÉ

#### SCRIPT TYPE 1 : PROBLEM-SOLUTION VSL (15-20 MIN)
**Structure :**
1. Hook (pattern interrupt) — 1 min
2. Story/credibility — 3 min
3. Problem agitation — 4 min
4. Solution reveal — 3 min
5. Offer presentation — 4 min
6. Urgency/scarcity — 2 min
7. CTA — 1 min

#### SCRIPT TYPE 2 : SHORT VSL (5-7 MIN)
**Pour warm traffic :**
1. Quick hook — 30 sec
2. Result promise — 1 min
3. How it works — 2 min
4. Social proof — 1 min
5. Offer + CTA — 2 min

#### SCRIPT TYPE 3 : REGISTRATION VSL (2-3 MIN)
**Pour opt-in pages :**
1. Hook (problem) — 30 sec
2. Promise (solution) — 1 min
3. What they'll get — 1 min
4. CTA (register) — 30 sec

#### SCRIPT TYPE 4 : TESTIMONIAL-BASED VSL
**Social proof driven :**
1. Hook with results — 1 min
2. Multiple testimonials — 8 min
3. Offer presentation — 3 min
4. Guarantee — 1 min
5. CTA — 1 min

### 💡 PRINCIPES CLÉS

1. **Script word-for-word** — Pas d'improvisation
2. **Practice before recording** — 5+ run-throughs minimum
3. **Energy is everything** — Ton doit matcher message
4. **Visuals support, don't distract** — Background simple
5. **CTA clear and repeated** — Dire exactement quoi faire

### 🛠️ APPLICATION PRATIQUE

**Creating your VSL :**
1. Choisir script type selon funnel
2. Adapter avec détails produit
3. Rehearse 5-10 fois
4. Record with good lighting/audio
5. Edit pour pacing (enlever dead time)

## 57. LANDING PAGE TEMPLATES COLLECTION

### 📖 INFORMATIONS
- **Auteur :** ACQ / Alex Hormozi
- **Année :** 2024
- **Pages :** ~50 (templates)
- **Priorité :** 🟡 MEDIUM
- **Type :** Templates

### 🎯 CONCEPT CENTRAL
> "La landing page est le premier point de contact. Elle doit convertir, pas juste informer."

Collection de templates de landing pages testés et optimisés pour différents types d'offres.

### 📚 CONTENU DÉTAILLÉ

#### TEMPLATE 1 : REGISTRATION PAGE
**Pour book launches / lead magnets :**
- Hero section with strong headline
- Problem-agitation-solution
- Video/VSL integration
- Simple opt-in form
- Social proof bar
- FAQ section

#### TEMPLATE 2 : SALES PAGE (LONG-FORM)
**Pour courses / high-ticket :**
- Attention-grabbing headline
- Story/opening
- Problem amplification
- Solution presentation
- Offer stack with value
- Testimonials (before/after)
- FAQ and objections
- Guarantee and CTA

#### TEMPLATE 3 : WEBINAR REGISTRATION
**Pour funnel webinar :**
- Date/time urgency
- Topic/promise headline
- Speaker credibility
- What you'll learn (bullet points)
- Registration form
- Replay mention (if applicable)

#### TEMPLATE 4 : APPLICATION PAGE
**Pour high-ticket services :**
- Qualification headline
- "Apply to work with us"
- Who it's for / not for
- Process explanation
- Application form (qualifying questions)
- Expectation setting

### 💡 PRINCIPES CLÉS

1. **One page, one goal** — Pas de distractions
2. **Above the fold matters most** — 80% ne scrollent pas
3. **Mobile-first** — 60%+ traffic mobile
4. **Load speed = conversion** — Chaque seconde compte
5. **Test everything** — A/B test headlines, CTAs, colors

### 🛠️ APPLICATION PRATIQUE

**Using the templates :**
1. Choisir template selon type d'offre
2. Copier structure, adapter copy
3. Customiser design (brand colors)
4. Implementer tracking (pixels)
5. A/B test variations

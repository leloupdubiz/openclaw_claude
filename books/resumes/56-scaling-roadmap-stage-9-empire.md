## 56. SCALING ROADMAP STAGE 9 : EMPIRE

### 📖 INFORMATIONS
- **Auteur :** Alex Hormozi (ACQ)
- **Année :** 2024
- **Pages :** ~15
- **Priorité :** 🟢 LOW
- **Type :** Conglomerate

### 🎯 CONCEPT CENTRAL
> "Stage 9 = Empire. Multiple businesses, investissements, influence. Le summum de l'entrepreneuriat."

Focus : Construction d'un portefeuille diversifié d'entreprises et d'investissements.

### 📚 CONTENU DÉTAILLÉ

#### SECTION 1 : HOLDING COMPANY STRUCTURE
**Structure holding :**
- Multiple operating companies
- Shared services (finance, legal, HR)
- Capital allocation entre entities
- Risk diversification
- Tax optimization

#### SECTION 2 : INVESTMENT STRATEGY
**Beyond operating businesses :**
- Venture capital (startups)
- Private equity (mature companies)
- Real estate
- Public markets
- Alternative assets

#### SECTION 3 : PORTFOLIO MANAGEMENT
**Gestion multi-entités :**
- Board oversight
- CEO selection/development
- Capital allocation discipline
- Performance metrics
- Synergies identification

#### SECTION 4 : FAMILY OFFICE
**Wealth management :**
- Estate planning
- Next-gen education
- Philanthropy coordination
- Lifestyle management
- Legacy preservation

### 💡 PRINCIPES CLÉS

1. **Diversification** — Pas tout dans une seule entreprise
2. **Operator-investor mindset** — Créer ET investir
3. **Discipline capital allocation** — Où l'argent travaille le mieux ?
4. **Develop talent** — Grands leaders pour chaque business
5. **Play infinite game** — Pas de fin, juste continuer à jouer

### 🛠️ APPLICATION PRATIQUE

**Empire Building :**
1. Structurer holding company
2. Identifier 2-3 nouvelles opportunités business
3. Démarrer investment activities (angel investing)
4. Hire family office team
5. Créer board of advisors

### 📊 EXEMPLE CONCRET

**Stage 9 Empire :**
- **Holdings :** 4 operating companies, 2 investments
- **Sectors :** SaaS, e-commerce, media, real estate
- **Team :** Shared services (20), company teams (200+)
- **Investments :** 15 startups, 3 PE deals
- **Role :** Founder = investor/strategist, pas operator

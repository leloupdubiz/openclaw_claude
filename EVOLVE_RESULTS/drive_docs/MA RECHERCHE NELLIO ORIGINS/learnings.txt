How to review videos
The Cheat Sheet
Questions to ask yourself before submitting your brief for video ads
	•	Does the first 1-2 seconds of this ad hook the RIGHT user in & KEEP them hooked in?
	•	Is this a video that your potential customers would want to see?
	•	Does the copywriting create a clear image / visual in the user's mind?
	•	Does this video feel native to the platform?
	•	Does this video contain a label for who the type of person is?
	•	Would this video make someone smile or laugh?
	•	Is the video relatable?
	•	Does this video actually look like an ad?
	•	Gut check - does this video feel authentic?
	•	What learnings of previous winning ads have we applied to this ad?
	•	Does the ad educate the user in a creative way on how to achieve their core desire?
	•	Does this video tell a story your ideal customer would find interesting?
	•	Have you applied escalation to exaggerate your claim in a clear and relatable way?
	•	Does this ad separate your brand from the competition?
